Rohit Mukherjee Portfolio

A storytelling-focused portfolio for an advertising creative.

Rohit's portfolio is a crafted storytelling experience that places narrative above noise. The design emphasizes a sequence of moments — hero imagery, detailed case study pages, and supporting process write-ups — that invite visitors to follow the creative journey. Each section in the portfolio was refined to balance strong visuals with readable typography, ensuring the work itself remains the star while the layout supports clarity and pace.

The project required designing a modular system that would scale with different campaigns and deliverables. I created reusable layout blocks to showcase print, digital, and campaign case studies while keeping the load times and responsiveness tightly optimized. Visual hierarchy was tuned for both desktop and mobile visitors, ensuring the emotional impact translates across devices.

To communicate Rohit’s creative practice, I focused on custom typography scale, subtle animated reveals, and an editorial-like grid system. The result is a confident portfolio that feels like a magazine feature — connective, directional, and focused on the narrative of each project rather than a flat gallery of images.

Amisha — Design Portfolio

A clean, expressive portfolio for a graphic designer.

Amisha’s portfolio project is rooted in showcasing visual craft and process. I designed a restrained interface where large imagery and precise spacing take center stage, allowing each piece of work to breathe. The layout supports multiple formats — editorial images, brand systems, and small interactive prototypes — while maintaining a single coherent voice.

Attention to detail was crucial: color swatches, typographic treatments, and contextual notes accompany each piece. I implemented a lightweight CMS-friendly structure so the portfolio can be expanded without breaking the visual rhythm. The interactions were intentionally subtle — tilt effects, soft fades, and context-aware captions that enhance, not distract.

The end result is an expressive portfolio that reads as a curated exhibition of Amisha's best work. The design gives editors and clients a focused view into the creative thinking and execution behind each project, encouraging higher-quality leads and confident presentations.

MakeUp Artist Sample Site

A soft, aesthetic portfolio designed for beauty professionals.

This makeup portfolio is built around delicate rhythm and tactile visual language: soft gradients, carefully chosen imagery, and an emphasis on detail that communicates luxury and approachability. The design supports high-resolution photography while preserving fast load times through optimized assets and considered layout choices.

I structured the case study to highlight services, pricing, and a gallery of looks. The goal was to convert browsers into clients by showcasing before/after imagery, a clear services list, and a straightforward booking CTA. The design intentionally balances editorial imagery and clear information hierarchy.

Micro-interactions were added to enhance the sensory feel: hover scales on portfolio items, gentle fades on overlays, and a refined typography scale tuned for mobile and desktop. The outcome is a site that feels like a boutique experience — soft, memorable, and effective at driving bookings.
